# Cross-Border MVP Execution Index

This is the working index for the 4-8 week quick-validation independent ecommerce site.

## Working Assumptions

- Budget: RMB 10,000-30,000.
- Timeline: 4-8 weeks.
- Market: United States.
- Category angle: lightweight problem-solving lifestyle goods.
- Platform: SaaS ecommerce platform first, preferably Shopify or a cross-border SaaS with PayPal/card payment and pixel support.
- Main goal: validate whether at least one SKU can turn traffic into profitable orders.

## Files Created

1. `cross-border-mvp-market-and-products.md`
   - Market decision.
   - Category positioning.
   - SKU priority.
   - Competitor references.
   - First 7-day product actions.

2. `cross-border-mvp-supplier-validation.csv`
   - Supplier quote and sample tracker.
   - Required documents by SKU.
   - Quality checks, logistics plan, after-sales rules, and red flags.

3. `cross-border-mvp-supplier-email-template.md`
   - Supplier outreach message.
   - Follow-up questions.
   - Supplier rejection and shortlist rules.

4. `cross-border-mvp-site-brief.md`
   - SaaS site setup brief.
   - Required pages.
   - Product page drafts.
   - Payment, shipping, return, and tracking setup.
   - Pre-launch QA checklist.

5. `cross-border-mvp-traffic-test-plan.md`
   - Two-channel test plan.
   - Budget guardrails.
   - UTM convention.
   - Creative matrix.
   - 14-day traffic rhythm.

6. `cross-border-mvp-traffic-tracker.csv`
   - Daily performance tracker for spend, impressions, clicks, add-to-carts, checkouts, purchases, revenue, margin, refunds, and decisions.

7. `cross-border-mvp-go-pivot-stop-review.md`
   - Week 7-8 decision framework.
   - Go/Pivot/Stop thresholds.
   - SKU review template.
   - Final decision memo format.

## Execution Order

### Week 1

- Use `cross-border-mvp-market-and-products.md` to confirm SKU priority.
- Send supplier messages using `cross-border-mvp-supplier-email-template.md`.
- Fill supplier responses into `cross-border-mvp-supplier-validation.csv`.
- Order samples for cooling towels, pet hair remover, and heatless curl set first.

### Week 2

- Evaluate samples with the quality checks in `cross-border-mvp-supplier-validation.csv`.
- Confirm packed dimensions and logistics costs before any inventory commitment.
- Choose 3 launch SKUs and keep risky SKUs blocked until documents pass.

### Week 3-4

- Build the SaaS site using `cross-border-mvp-site-brief.md`.
- Add product pages, policies, payment, email templates, and analytics pixels.
- Run a complete test order before launch.

### Week 5-6

- Run the traffic plan in `cross-border-mvp-traffic-test-plan.md`.
- Record daily metrics in `cross-border-mvp-traffic-tracker.csv`.
- Change one variable at a time: creative, product page, offer, or audience.

### Week 7-8

- Use `cross-border-mvp-go-pivot-stop-review.md` to decide whether to continue, revise, or stop.
- Only scale spend if the winning SKU has real conversion or strong add-to-cart signal and controllable operations.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not order bulk inventory before sample quality, packed dimensions, and logistics costs are confirmed.
- Do not launch paid ads before pixels and test order are verified.
- Do not claim fast US shipping unless inventory and carrier data support it.
- Do not fabricate reviews, urgency, stock scarcity, or product claims.
- Do not continue spending when the data says the product is not working.
