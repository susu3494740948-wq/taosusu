# Supplier Outreach Template

Use this message for Alibaba, 1688 agents, existing factories, or trading companies. Replace bracketed fields before sending.

## Initial Message

Hello [Supplier Name],

We are preparing a small test order for the US market and would like to evaluate your product:

Product: [SKU name]
Target market: United States
Expected selling channel: independent ecommerce website
First test quantity: [quantity range]

Please send the following information:

1. Sample price and sample shipping cost to [city/country].
2. MOQ and unit price for 100, 300, 500, and 1,000 units.
3. Production lead time and restock lead time.
4. Packed product weight and packed dimensions.
5. Carton quantity, carton weight, and carton dimensions.
6. Product material, available colors, and packaging options.
7. Real product photos, packaging photos, and video demos.
8. Whether you can provide English label, English instruction card, barcode/UPC support, and logo sticker.
9. Recent purchase invoice or supply proof for source verification.
10. Any compliance documents required for the US market.

For battery or electronic products, please also provide MSDS/SDS, UN38.3, battery specifications, COC, safety mark photos, and English safety manual.

Before we place any bulk order, we will check sample quality, packaging quality, logistics cost, and documentation completeness.

Thank you,
[Your Name]

## Follow-Up After Quote

Hello [Supplier Name],

Thank you for the quote. Before we order samples, please confirm:

- Is the packed weight accurate for one retail-ready unit?
- Are the dimensions measured after final packaging?
- Can you send a short video showing the product function and packaging?
- Can you support replacement or credit for defective units found before shipping?
- Can you keep the same material and packaging for repeat orders?

If everything is confirmed, please send the sample payment link and estimated dispatch date.

## Supplier Decision Rules

Reject or pause a supplier when:

- They cannot provide packed weight and dimensions.
- They avoid compliance questions.
- Their product photos are only marketplace screenshots.
- They promise unrealistic shipping times or product effects.
- Their quote only works at high MOQ and does not fit the MVP budget.
- They cannot explain quality control or defect handling.

Shortlist a supplier when:

- They answer with specific numbers, photos, and documents.
- Their sample cost is reasonable.
- Their MOQ fits a 100-300 unit validation order.
- Their product has clear packaging and labeling options.
- They can support repeat orders within 7-20 days after validation.
