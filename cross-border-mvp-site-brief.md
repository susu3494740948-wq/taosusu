# Cross-Border Independent Site MVP Brief

## Platform Decision

Recommended MVP platform: Shopify or a China-based cross-border SaaS with PayPal, card payment, tracking pixels, policy pages, and abandoned checkout email support.

Use SaaS first because the validation goal is orders and learning speed, not custom engineering. Do not self-build the commerce stack before product-market-channel signals are clear.

## Store Positioning

Working store concept: practical summer, travel, home, and pet fixes for everyday US households.

Tone: clear, useful, honest, and demonstration-led. Avoid exaggerated claims, luxury positioning, medical claims, or "miracle" language.

Suggested domain style:

- Short, easy to spell, broad enough for the first category cluster.
- Avoid product-specific domains such as `coolingtowelonly.com` because the MVP may pivot.
- Example naming directions: `DailyFixKit`, `SunnyHomeKit`, `EverydayReadyCo`, `PracticalNest`.

## Required Pages

### Home Page

Purpose: explain the store in under 5 seconds and route users to hero products.

Sections:

1. Hero banner: "Everyday fixes for hot days, messy homes, travel bags, and pet hair."
2. Featured product cards: cooling towel, pet hair remover, heatless curl set.
3. Proof-led section: short demos, before/after visuals, use cases.
4. Trust strip: secure checkout, tracked shipping, 30-day issue support, responsive email support.
5. FAQ preview: shipping time, returns, order tracking.

### Collection Page

Initial collections:

- Summer Comfort
- Pet Cleaning
- Travel Organization
- Hair And Beauty Tools

Keep navigation small. Do not create empty collections.

### Product Page Template

Required sections:

1. Product title with the problem solved.
2. 3-5 image/video assets showing real use.
3. Price, discount if used, shipping note.
4. Bullet benefits.
5. "How to use" section.
6. "What's included" section.
7. Shipping and returns summary.
8. FAQ specific to the product.
9. Reviews or "Customer testing in progress" only if truthful. Do not fabricate reviews.

## Product Page Drafts

### Cooling Towel 4-Pack With Pouch

Title: Cooling Towel 4-Pack With Carry Pouch

Short description:
Stay cooler during workouts, outdoor games, theme parks, and hot commutes. Wet it, wring it, snap it, and wear it around your neck or pack it in your summer bag.

Benefit bullets:

- Lightweight cooling towel for gym, running, hiking, sports, and travel.
- Includes carry pouch for bags, cars, lockers, or outdoor events.
- Reusable and machine washable.
- Easy to demo in short videos: wet, snap, wear.

Important wording:

- Say "helps you feel cooler" instead of promising a specific temperature drop.
- Include exact dimensions after supplier confirmation.

### Reusable Pet Hair Remover

Title: Reusable Pet Hair Remover For Sofas, Cars, And Blankets

Short description:
Clean up everyday pet hair without sticky refill sheets. Roll across fabric surfaces, collect hair in the chamber, then empty and reuse.

Benefit bullets:

- Works on many fabric surfaces such as sofas, blankets, car seats, and pet beds.
- No disposable sticky refills.
- Reusable design for daily cleanup.
- Strong before/after demo potential.

Important wording:

- Do not claim it works on every fabric.
- Add "test gently on delicate fabric first."

### Heatless Satin Curl Set

Title: Heatless Satin Curl Set For Overnight Styling

Short description:
Wrap your hair before bed and wake up with soft curls without hot tools. A simple content-friendly beauty tool for low-effort morning routines.

Benefit bullets:

- No heat styling required.
- Soft satin-style surface for overnight wear.
- Includes curling rod, clip, and scrunchies depending on final supplier set.
- Best for medium to long hair.

Important wording:

- Do not claim salon results for every hair type.
- Only use "silk" if the supplier provides valid material proof.

## Checkout And Payment Setup

Minimum launch configuration:

- PayPal enabled.
- Card payment enabled if Stripe or another card processor is available.
- Store currency: USD.
- Test order completed before launch.
- Abandoned checkout email enabled.
- Order confirmation, shipping confirmation, and refund email templates reviewed.

Checkout trust requirements:

- No surprise shipping fees at the final step.
- Shipping times shown before checkout.
- Return policy linked in footer and checkout.
- Support email visible in footer and order emails.

## Shipping Policy MVP

Use conservative wording until supplier and logistics data are proven.

Suggested policy:

- Processing time: 2-5 business days.
- Estimated delivery to the United States: 7-15 business days after dispatch.
- Tracking number provided when available.
- Delays may occur during peak seasons, customs checks, weather events, or carrier disruptions.
- If an order is lost or significantly delayed, customers can contact support for review.

Do not promise 3-5 day US delivery unless inventory is already in a US warehouse.

## Return And Refund Policy MVP

Suggested policy:

- Customers can contact support within 30 days of delivery for damaged, defective, missing, or incorrect items.
- Photo or video evidence may be required.
- For hygiene-sensitive beauty items, opened/used returns should be limited unless defective.
- For buyer's remorse returns, customer may be responsible for return shipping if accepted.
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method after approval.

## Tracking And Analytics Setup

Install before paid traffic:

- GA4.
- Meta Pixel.
- TikTok Pixel.
- Google Search Console.
- UTM naming convention for every ad and organic campaign.
- Email capture or abandoned checkout tool.

Core events to verify:

- Page view.
- View content/product view.
- Add to cart.
- Begin checkout.
- Purchase.

## Pre-Launch QA Checklist

- Mobile homepage loads quickly and explains the offer within 5 seconds.
- Product pages have real photos or truthful sample photos.
- Add-to-cart works.
- Discount code works if used.
- PayPal/card payment test works.
- Order confirmation email is received.
- Shipping policy, return policy, privacy policy, terms, contact page, and FAQ are linked in footer.
- Product claims are conservative and match supplier proof.
- All ads and pixels fire correctly.
- No fake reviews, fake countdowns, or unsupported "limited stock" claims.
