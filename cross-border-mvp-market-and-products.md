# Cross-Border MVP Market And Product Decision

## Decision

Primary market: United States.

Store angle: summer comfort, home organization, travel readiness, and pet cleaning products that solve visible everyday problems in 5-15 seconds of video.

Initial category focus: lightweight problem-solving lifestyle goods. Avoid broad general merchandise, regulated goods, food, supplements, medical claims, children's safety products, counterfeit IP, and products with fragile fulfillment economics.

Primary launch hypothesis:

- US buyers will respond to low-risk, visually demonstrable products in the USD 19.99-29.99 range.
- The first independent site should start as a focused "practical summer and home fixes" store, not a full lifestyle brand.
- The first hero product should be easy to explain, light to ship, low-return, and cheap enough for impulse purchase.

## Competitor References

### Cooling Towel 4-Pack With Pouch

- Amazon references: Sukeen Cooling Towels, CSITE Cooling Towels, YQXCC Cooling Towels.
- Typical price anchor: $14.99-$19.99.
- Differentiation needed: better pouch, clearer use cases, bundle positioning, stronger photos, and "summer go bag" content.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: thin material, weak cooling effect, color bleed, bad smell, small size.

### Reusable Pet Hair Remover

- Amazon references: ChomChom Roller, Magic Pet Hair Remover, reusable lint brushes.
- Typical price anchor: $15.99-$29.99.
- Differentiation needed: reusable/no refill message, multiple fabric demos, clear before/after proof.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: scratching fabric, weak effect on short hair, hard-to-clean chamber, flimsy handle.

### Heatless Satin Curl Set

- Amazon/retail references: Kitsch Heatless Curling Set, Lilysilk Silk Curler, satin curling rods.
- Typical price anchor: $14.00-$39.00.
- Differentiation needed: beginner tutorial, sleep comfort, hair type examples, bundle with clip/scrunchies.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: slipping overnight, poor results on short hair, fake silk claim, color bleed.

### Compression Packing Cubes Set

- Amazon references: BAGSMART, Gonex, BAGAIL.
- Typical price anchor: $20.00-$43.00.
- Differentiation needed: zipper proof, compression demo, clean color palette, carry-on challenge.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: zipper failure, thin fabric, wrong sizing, no real compression.

### USB-C Bladeless Neck Fan

- Amazon references: JISULIFE, CIVPOWER, Kinhowly.
- Typical price anchor: $25.00-$39.99.
- Differentiation needed: noise test, battery honesty, heat-wave scenarios.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: overstated battery life, weak airflow, loud motor, missing battery compliance documents.

### Clear Acrylic Vanity Organizer

- Amazon/retail references: Sorbus, Target drawer organizers, Casafield.
- Typical price anchor: $24.99-$39.99.
- Differentiation needed: anti-break packaging, clearer dimensions, small-space use cases.
- Buyer pain points to avoid: cracking, scratches, high dimensional weight, drawer jams.

## First 7-Day Actions

1. Request samples and quotes from 3 suppliers each for the first 4 SKUs.
2. Ask for real packaging weight, packed dimensions, English labels, and production lead time.
3. Order samples only for cooling towels, pet hair remover, and heatless curl set first.
4. Keep the neck fan on hold until supplier provides MSDS/SDS, UN38.3, battery specification, and safety mark photos.
5. Do not buy acrylic organizer inventory until packaging drop-test proof and shipping quotes are available.
