# 评论 Inbox 分类与英文回复 SOP

> **练习项目说明**：以下评论与回复均为模拟场景，用于展示跨境客服执行能力。

---

## 一、评论分类规则

| 分类 | 识别特征 | 处理优先级 | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| 物流慢 | shipping, delivery, still waiting, 2 weeks | 高（24h 内回复） | 超过 SLA 15 天 → 主管 |
| 质量问题 | broken, defective, doesn't work, poor quality | 高 | 一律 escalation 主管 |
| 尺寸不符 | smaller than expected, wrong size, not as described | 中 | 连续 3 条同类 → 主管 |
| 产品咨询 | how to use, does it work on X, is this reusable | 中 | 自行处理 |
| 正面评价 | love it, works great, highly recommend | 低 | 自行感谢 |
| 恶意/无关 | spam, competitor attack, off-topic | 低 | 标记 + 上报平台 |

---

## 二、回复原则

1. **先道歉再解释**（物流/质量类），避免争辩
2. **给出具体下一步**（查追踪号 / 补发 / 退款申请链接）
3. **不承诺平台政策外权益**（如超 30 天全额退款）
4. **英文简洁**，避免过长段落
5. **涉及退款/补发/产品质量 → escalation 主管**

## 三、5 组英文差评 + 回复样例

### 1. 物流慢（TikTok Shop · Cooling Towel）

**Customer review (1★)**：
> Ordered 2 weeks ago and still no update. Tracking hasn't moved in 5 days. Very disappointed.

**分类**：物流慢

**Escalation**：否（仍在 15 天 SLA 内，先回复 + 查追踪）

**Reply**：
> Hi, we're sorry for the delay and understand your frustration. We've checked your order — it's currently in transit to the US distribution hub. International shipping can take 7–12 business days after dispatch. We've sent you a DM with the latest tracking link. If there's no update within 3 business days, please reply here and we'll escalate to our logistics team. Thank you for your patience.

---

### 2. 质量问题（Temu · Pet Hair Remover）

**Customer review (1★)**：
> The roller stopped working after 3 uses. Waste of money.

**分类**：质量问题

**Escalation**：是 → 主管（可能涉及批次召回或退款审批）

**Reply**：
> Hi, we're sorry the product didn't meet your expectations. This isn't the experience we want for our customers. We've forwarded your case to our support team (ref: QC-20260701). Please check your email within 24 hours for a replacement or refund option. Thank you for letting us know.

**Internal note**：记录批次号、订单号，通知主管 @ops-lead

---

### 3. 尺寸不符（Shopee · Cooling Towel）

**Customer review (2★)**：
> Towels are smaller than I expected from the photos. Misleading.

**分类**：尺寸不符

**Escalation**：否（首次，但需标记 Listing 优化）

**Reply**：
> Hi, thank you for your feedback. Each towel measures approximately 40×12 inches when unfolded — we've added this to the product description to avoid confusion. If the size doesn't work for you, please contact us via Shopee chat within 7 days and we'll help with a return per our store policy. We appreciate you helping us improve our listing.

**Follow-up**：提交 Listing 优化建议给主管（主图加尺寸参照）

---

### 4. 产品咨询（TikTok 评论区）

**Comment**：
> Will this work on leather car seats or only fabric?

**分类**：产品咨询

**Escalation**：否

**Reply**：
> Great question! The roller works best on fabric surfaces (sofa, car fabric seats, blankets). For leather, we recommend testing a small hidden area first — it may not pick up as effectively. Let us know if you need more details!

---

### 5. 恶意/夸大差评（独立站 · Heatless Curl Set）

**Customer review (1★)**：
> This product caused hair loss and burns!!! Do not buy!!!

**分类**：恶意/夸大（无具体订单信息，用语极端）

**Escalation**：是 → 主管 + 平台申诉

**Reply**（公开）：
> Hi, we take product safety seriously. Our heatless curl set contains no heating elements. We'd like to investigate — please email support@taosusu.com with your order number so we can review. If we can't verify a purchase, we may request the review be removed per platform guidelines.

**Internal note**：查订单库确认是否真实买家；若非真实订单，准备平台申诉材料
